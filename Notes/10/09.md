### Properties  

- Reflexive - a = a  
- Symmetric - a + b = b + a   
- Transitive - a = b, b = c, a = c  

|Reflexive|
|:-:|  
$\forall x \in A$, $\mathbb{_x}$ $R$ $\mathbb{_x}$  
Every element in A is related to itself.  

|Anti-Reflexive|
|:-:|
$\forall x \in A$, $\neg \mathbb{_x}$ $R$  $\mathbb{_x}$

|Symmetric|
|:-:|
